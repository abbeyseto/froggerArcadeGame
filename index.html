Welcome to Abiodun's Frogger Arcade Game

Game Play

Aim is to get to the blue water tiles without being touched by bugs.
For each successful trip to the water tiles, you score 5 points and the game level/difficulty is increased by one.
For each time you are hit by a bug, you loose 0.5 point and you are taken back to the starting point.

Navigation

For touch screen devices You can swipe up/down/left/right to move across the road...
For laptop computers, use the arow up/down/left/right keys to navigate
